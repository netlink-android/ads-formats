HTML5 Interactive Ads

An interactive ad is an online advertisement that allows users to interact with the ad through actions like clicking, scrolling, hovering, or dragging. This is different from traditional ads that only allow users to view them without direct interaction.

HTML5 Carousel Ads

Carousel ads are often used to promote products or services, showcase features, or tell a brand story. The images or videos are arranged in a logical order to help customers better understand the product or service being advertised.

HTML5 Expandable Ads

An expandable ad is an online advertisement that allows users to expand the ad to view additional content or interact with the product through actions like clicking or dragging. This allows the ad to display more detailed information and create an interactive experience for users.
